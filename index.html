Education, Accomplishments, Talent

I first began my software development education at Cincinnati State where I was pursuing a bachelor's in software engineering. After realizing all of the new and different resources becoming available I quickly changed my path to self learning. My eyes really opened after understanding how much hard work went behind everything on the web. This became a new life goal of mine, to be able to work through difficult logic problems and create software of my own for the world to use. This all led me to MAX training where I could finally polish my skills to become a productive member of the software development sector. With natural strengths of learner and restorative and having gained education in Java, C#, SQL and JavaScript along with frameworks spring and angular in addition to a newly defined career focus I am now ready to launch my career as a software developer.
