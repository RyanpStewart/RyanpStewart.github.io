Education, Accomplishments, Talent

Hi, my name is Ryan Stewart. My education in software development first began at Cincinnati State back in 2013 where I was pursuing a bachelor's degree in Software Engineering. After realizing the number of resources quickly becoming available online, I opted to take a different approach of self-learning while working food service jobs. Around that time I decided to build my own PC. This really opened my eyes to the huge spectrum of technology out in the world. Everything from knowing how a computer operated, to the code that goes behind the websites we all love to use was essential for my personal growth and well-being. It really confirmed that my life goal was to pursue this career path. Following my experimental coding phase I quickly realized this is the perfect time for a career change into something I am truly passionate about. This led me to join MAX Training boot camp to solidify my skills to finally become a productive member of the software development sector. Solving problems with software has always been a major interest of mine and I'm always looking for unique viable ways to resolve new problems. With natural strengths of learner and restorative and having gained education in Java, C#, SQL and JavaScript, along with frameworks Spring and Angular in addition to a newly clearly defined career focus, I am now ready to launch my career as a Software Developer.
