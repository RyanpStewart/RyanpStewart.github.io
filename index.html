Education, Accomplishments, Talent

Hi, my name is Ryan Stewart.

I embarked on my journey in software development when I enrolled at Cincinnati State in 2013 to pursue a bachelor's degree in Software Engineering. However, as I discovered the wealth of online learning resources available, I decided to take a different path and began self-learning while working in the food service industry.

During this time, I took on the challenge of building my own PC, which exposed me to the vast world of technology. Understanding how computers operate and the intricate code behind the websites we use every day became essential for my personal growth and ignited my passion for a career in this field.

Through my exploration of coding, I realized that this was the perfect opportunity for a career change into something I am truly passionate about. This realization motivated me to join MAX Training boot camp to further develop my skills and become a valuable asset in the software development sector.

Problem-solving through software has always been a major interest of mine, and I continuously seek innovative and effective ways to tackle new challenges. With a natural inclination for learning and restoration, combined with my education in Java, C#, SQL, and JavaScript, as well as experience with frameworks such as Spring and Angular, I have now solidified my career focus as a Software Developer.

I am excited to launch my career in software development, leveraging my strengths and acquired knowledge to create impactful solutions and contribute to the growth of innovative projects.
